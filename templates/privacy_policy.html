Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to PrepAI. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our AI voice roleplay platform.

By using PrepAI, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Profile information (optional)

2.2 Voice and Audio Data

Microphone Access:

PrepAI requires microphone access to enable voice-based roleplay conversations with AI bots. Your audio is processed in real-time to provide interactive voice responses. We do not store or record your voice conversations unless explicitly stated for specific features.

When you use voice roleplay features:

  • Audio is transmitted securely to our AI processing servers
  • Voice data is processed in real-time and is NOT permanently stored
  • Temporary audio buffers are immediately deleted after processing
  • We do not create permanent recordings of your conversations
  • You can revoke microphone access at any time through your browser settings

2.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Session duration and frequency
  • Bot interactions and preferences
  • Credit usage and transaction history
  • Device information and browser type
  • IP address and general location (city/country level)

2.4 Payment Information

For credit purchases:

  • Payment processing is handled by secure third-party providers (Razorpay)
  • We do NOT store credit card numbers or banking details
  • We only retain transaction IDs and payment status

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide Services: Enable AI voice roleplay interactions and process conversations
  • Improve Quality: Analyze usage patterns to enhance bot performance and user experience
  • Account Management: Manage your credits, subscriptions, and earnings
  • Communication: Send important updates, notifications, and support responses
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and technical issues
  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal obligations and enforce our terms of service

4. Data Security

Your Security Matters:

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits.

Our security measures include:

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure Storage: Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access to user data
  • Regular Audits: Ongoing security assessments and updates
  • Real-time Processing: Voice data is processed in memory and not stored permanently

5. Microphone Permissions

Why We Need Microphone Access

PrepAI is a voice-based AI roleplay platform. Microphone access is essential for:

  • Real-time voice conversation with AI bots
  • Speech recognition and natural language processing
  • Providing interactive voice responses

How Your Voice Data is Handled

  • Real-Time Only: Audio is processed instantly and not recorded
  • No Permanent Storage: We do not save voice recordings to our servers
  • Secure Transmission: Voice data is encrypted during transmission
  • Your Control: You can deny or revoke microphone access anytime
  • Session-Based: Audio access is only active during roleplay sessions
Browser Controls:

You maintain full control over microphone permissions through your browser settings. Denying access will prevent voice features from working but will not affect other platform features.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate (hosting, payment processing, AI services)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Safety and Security: To protect users' rights, property, or safety
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your login and preferences
  • Analyze site usage and performance
  • Provide personalized content

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Microphone Control: Revoke microphone access at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at founder@prepai.online
